Sample Letter

Prefer to send your own email? Use the sample letter below.

Dear Councillor, I am a resident writing to urge you to support responsible investment in Merton’s pension fund, in line with international human rights standards. Please encourage the Pensions Committee to contact other councils that share the same LCIV sub-funds as Merton to work together on investment options that avoid companies linked to serious human rights abuses. Other councils have already taken this approach successfully, including councils in LCIV's PEPPA sub-fund. I also request that Merton’s Responsible Investment Policy be updated to exclude companies involved in war crimes, crimes against humanity, apartheid, illegal settlements, or those providing arms, finance, products or services that enable such abuses anywhere in the world, including companies operating in or supporting Israel’s international law violations in Palestine. Finally, I ask that all investments be reviewed annually using credible sources such as the UN OHCHR report, the Who Profits database, and the AFSC Investigate watchlist. Your decision on this issue will influence how I vote in May. Regards,
